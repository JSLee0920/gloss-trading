# F8 — Analyst and Social (overview)

Phase 6. Split into two notes once the brief requirements were mapped in full:

- [[Market Analysis]] — move explanations, technical patterns, news, sentiment, personalised briefs
- [[Content Studio]] — personas, platform-adapted posts, calendar, anonymisation gate

## Why still last

Both are growth features bolted onto a product that must already work. Content generation demos well and retains nobody. The trigger for building either is evidence that the Phase 1–4 loop brings users back.

## Shared constraints

- No predictions, no signals, no profit claims — see [[Non-Goals]]
- Nothing generated from a user's data is published without anonymisation and human approval
