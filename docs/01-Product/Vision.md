# Vision

Trading platforms provide execution but not intelligence. Retail traders get fills; they do not get an analyst explaining the market, a coach watching their behaviour, or a tutor teaching from what they actually did.

Gloss collapses all three into one system, because all three run on the same substrate: **your own trade history**.

## The one-line pitch

Your trades teach three audiences at once — the analyst tells you what the market did, the coach tells you what *you* did, and the tutor turns the gap between them into a lesson you retain.

## Why this is one product, not three

A behavioural pattern is only meaningful against market context, and a lesson is only memorable when it uses a trade you lived through. Split them and each half is weaker.
